About Zero

One company.
Seven ways to build the future.

We started as a two-person studio in 2019. Today, ZERO is a house of independent divisions — each with its own team, craft, and clients — united by one operating standard and one name behind them.

01

Craft over shortcuts

Every division ships work it would put its own name on, not just ours.

02

Independence, shared standard

Each company runs its own roadmap, backed by the same operating principles.

03

Built to compound

Divisions refer, support, and staff each other — clients get a bigger bench than the brief.

"We didn't want to build one company that did everything adequately. We wanted seven companies that were each the best at one thing."
Amara Osei
Founder & CEO, ZERO
